Data Visualization

Neon Analytics Dashboard

A high-performance, real-time analytics platform designed to process and visualize over 1 million events per second with sub-50ms latency.

The Challenge

Traditional analytics dashboards struggle with massive data throughput. Our client needed a solution that could ingest raw stream data from IoT devices and render actionable insights without the typical "loading spinner" delay.

The Solution

We utilized WebSockets for the transport layer and implemented a custom rendering engine using WebGL. By bypassing the DOM for data-heavy charts, we achieved a consistent 60fps even under heavy load.

  • ✓ Reduced latency by 85% compared to previous solution.
  • ✓ Scalable to 10k concurrent users.
  • ✓ Dark mode first design system.
